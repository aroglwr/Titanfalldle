About
Every day a weapon from Titanfall 2 will be chosen at random and it is your job to guess it! With each guess you will get traits that it shares with the correct answer until you get it right!
Details
This is a goofy game inspired by LoLdle, Minecraftle and of course Wordle. You can view the project on GitHub.
Also check out Skylandle by me!
Privacy
There are no trackers or ads on this website and all hyperlinks are written such that the website they direct to does not hear from me. I use a single (1) cookie to keep guesses after refresh (you can check this in your browser's web tools) and are not saved by me.
